Weather

by aircall
Installed!

The Weather app is now installed on your Aircall phone!

It will fetch weather information from Australia, Belgium, Brazil, Canada, France, Germany, Great Britain, Ireland, Itlay, Mexico, Morocco, Netherlands, Spain, Sweden, Switzerland and USA.

Next step: configure the Numbers you want your integration to be linked on in your Aircall Dashboard! Only agents with the associated numbers will see weather insights.

Note: the Weather app will do its best to match a city/region with each of your contacts' phone number. If a phone number can't be geo-located (like mobile, toll-free...), the Weather card won't be displayed during the call. Available countries are listed above and below!
